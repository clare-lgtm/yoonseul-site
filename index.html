About

YOONSEUL INTERNATIONAL is a Korea-founded beauty company dedicated to introducing thoughtfully curated Korean skincare brands to Southeast Asia, starting with Malaysia.

Built on a deep understanding of the Korean beauty value chain across manufacturing, brand development, and distribution, the company was founded on a clear belief.

Great products alone are not enough. Brand value must be accurately translated, positioned, and understood in each local market.

Read More expand_more

Rather than pursuing volume-driven distribution, YOONSEUL INTERNATIONAL takes a selective and long-term approach. We collaborate with Korean brands that demonstrate strong product integrity, clear formulation logic, and sustainable growth potential, and introduce them through carefully designed market entry strategies tailored to local consumer behavior, cultural context, and channel dynamics.

Mission

To translate the true value of Korean beauty brands into the language of global markets.

Vision

To build long-term trust for Korean beauty brands in Southeast Asia through thoughtful distribution and brand stewardship.

Founder Introduction

The founder brings extensive hands-on experience across the Korean beauty industry, spanning brand development, product planning, and distribution.

With a strong foundation in the Korean market and on-the-ground insight into Southeast Asia, she has developed a clear perspective on how Korean beauty brands should be introduced, positioned, and sustained overseas.

Her focus is on building a structure in which product-driven Korean beauty brands can be properly understood, trusted, and embraced by consumers in each local market— beyond short-term trends and surface-level exposure.
